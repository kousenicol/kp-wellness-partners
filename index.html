Magnetic Electric TENS Machine

Advanced Magnetic Electro-Stimulation Device

What It Is

A proprietary Korean-developed device that combines pulsed electromagnetic field (PEMF) therapy with traditional electro-acupuncture stimulation (TENS). The magnetic field penetrates deeper than standard TENS units, targeting the meridian points with both electrical and magnetic energy simultaneously.

Key Benefits

  • Deep tissue pain relief (2–3× deeper than TENS)
  • Stimulates nerve regeneration & muscle recovery
  • Reduces muscle spasm & chronic inflammation
  • Improves lymphatic & circulatory flow
  • Non-invasive — used alongside or without needles

Certified and approved in South Korea. Combined use with ferromagnetic needles delivers a uniquely powerful synergistic treatment effect unmatched by conventional methods.
